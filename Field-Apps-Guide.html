🏭 Open TRAC Belt

Belt conveyor scale calibration. Records test rounds, auto-calculates % error, and generates professional PDF reports for customers and plant managers.

Result Status Reference
PASS

% Error < 0.5%
Scale is within spec.

MARGINAL

% Error 0.5–1.0%
Adjusted but watch it.

FAIL

% Error ≥ 1.0% — Scale is out of spec. Adjustment required. Document and notify plant manager.

7-Step Calibration Walkthrough
1
Visit Info

Select the scale from your library (or add a new one). Enter tech name, date, and any pre-visit notes.

💡 Scale library saves all your sites — you won't re-enter customer info each visit.
2
Belt Speed

Enter the belt speed in FPM and idler spacing in inches. The app calculates TWPF (test weight per foot) automatically.

3
Zero As Found

Record the zero reading before any adjustment. Use the ± Neg button if the reading is negative — do not type a minus sign.

💡 First round: set the Cal Span As Found here. The field is editable before any rounds are recorded.
4
Test Rounds

Enter start totalizer, run the belt, record end totalizer. Tap ▶ Start Timer to run the 5-minute countdown.

⚠️ Keep this screen open while the timer runs — leaving the page pauses the timer. The app will alert you if you navigate away.
💡 Record As Found first, then make adjustments and add rounds to confirm As Left.
5
Zero As Left

Record the zero reading after adjustment. Use ± Neg if negative.

6
Maintenance & Photos

Complete the maintenance checklist. Tap 📷 Add Photo to capture equipment photos — these embed directly in the Customer Report PDF.

💡 Photos work from your camera or existing gallery. No internet needed — stored on-device.
7
Review & Save

Review the full calibration summary. All rounds, % errors, and PASS/FAIL status display here. Save the visit, then generate your PDFs.

PDF Reports
📄
Customer Report

Full calibration certificate. Includes QR code, PASS/FAIL banner, test rounds table, and any photos taken. Give this to the customer.

🏗️
Site Summary

Plant manager one-pager. All scales tested in one visit, scorecard, pass/fail totals, and signature lines.

Generating PDFs From the visit summary screen, tap 📄 Customer Report or 🏗️ Site Summary. Your phone's share sheet will open — save as PDF or send directly via email or text.
Calibration Reminders

The home screen shows reminder cards for scales with upcoming or overdue calibration dates. Tap Draft Reminder Email on any site to generate a pre-written email covering all due scales at that location.

⚡ Open TRAC Cell

Load cell OHM testing. Records 13 readings per cell, flags cross-bridge deviations, calculates ROC, and generates a professional Customer Report PDF.

Cell Status Reference
PASS

All readings within spec. 10+ readings complete.

REVIEW

Cross-bridge deviation >1% from average. Flag for closer look.

FAIL

Negative resistance or mV reading detected — physically invalid.

PARTIAL

Fewer than 10 readings entered for this cell.

Job Setup
🏭
Customer & Scale Info

Enter customer name, scale ID, tech name, date, excitation voltage, mV/V rating, test weight, and cell capacity.

🎨
Wire Color Profiles

Set wire colors (+EX, −EX, +SIG, −SIG, Shield) for the load cell model. The app saves this profile — it auto-loads next time you test the same model.

🔄
Per-Cell Model Override

On mixed-manufacturer scales (e.g. 7 Mettler + 1 Rice Lake), tap Change ▾ on any cell to set a different model and wire colors for that cell only.

13-Reading Entry Flow
How it works Each cell has 13 sequential readings. Enter a value and tap Enter (or Next) to auto-advance. The app validates each reading in real time and shows a PASS/FAIL/SUSPECT badge instantly.
1
Excitation Resistance (Ω)

Resistance across excitation wires. Must be positive — negative auto-fails.

2
Signal Resistance (Ω)

Resistance across signal wires. Must be positive.

3–6
Cross-Bridge Readings (pp / pn / nn / np)

Four bridge readings. If any deviate >1% from their average, all four are flagged purple — cell status becomes REVIEW.

💡 Purple flag = cross-bridge imbalance. Not a hard fail, but document it.
7–8
−Comp / +Comp (Ω)

Compensation resistor readings. Must be positive.

9–10
Shorts — Ω and nS

Insulation check. Shorts Ω: any numeric value = FAIL (OL = PASS). Shorts nS: <0.5 = PASS, 0.5–1.0 = SUSPECT, >1.0 = BAD.

💡 Can't type "OL" on a number pad — use the OL button.
11
Zero Load (mV DC)

Must be between 0 and +0.5 mV. Negative = FAIL.

12
Dead Load (mV DC)

Must be positive. Negative = FAIL.

13
Known Load (mV DC)

ROC is auto-calculated: Known Load − Dead Load. Displayed in summary alongside the theoretical ROC from job setup.

Useful Shortcuts
📋
Copy Cell 1

On any cell other than Cell 1, tap Copy Cell 1 to paste all readings from the first cell. Useful on uniform junction boxes — confirm overwrite when prompted.

✏️
Edit from Summary

After completing a cell, tap the ✏ button on the summary row to go back and edit any reading. Returns you to summary when done — no data loss.

±
± Neg Button

Appears on decimal readings. Toggles a minus sign prefix. Use this instead of typing "−" — the phone keyboard won't let you type a negative on a number pad.

Customer Report PDF
Generating the report From the job summary screen, tap 📄 Customer Report. You'll be asked whether to include a signature block. The report includes a QR code, PASS/FAIL/REVIEW banner, all cells in a compact table with bridge deviation callouts, and ROC comparison.
Important — Your data lives on your device Both apps store all data in your phone's local browser storage. If you clear your browser data or switch phones, you'll lose your history unless you've made a backup.
Backup & Restore
📤
Export Backup

Tap the menu (≡) → Export Backup. Downloads a .json file with all your data. Save it to Files, iCloud, or email it to yourself. Do this regularly.

📥
Import / Restore

Tap Import Backup and select your .json file. The app merges the data — importing the same file twice is safe, no duplicates.

Sharing with Other Techs
📤
TRAC Belt — Share a Scale

On the scale detail screen, tap 📤 Share Scale. Sends a .json file with the scale info and all visit history. The receiving tech imports it and it appears in their library.

📤
TRAC Cell — Share a Job

From the job summary screen, tap 📤 Share Job. Exports the full job with all cell readings. The receiving tech imports and it appears in their history.

Getting the Apps on Your Phone
Add to Home Screen (iPhone) 1. Open the app link in Safari (not Chrome).
2. Tap the Share icon (box with arrow).
3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
4. The app icon appears on your home screen — works offline once added.
